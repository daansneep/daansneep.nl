PostgreSQL

I used Postgres for all my relational data solutions. After putting a lot of my time into data modeling I found working with postgres easy and intuitive. It was also very easy to connect my other applications to postgres.

MongoDB

I used Mongo as my first nosql document based database for a disclosed project where I had a vast amount of unrelated data. I connected this database to my rest-api and the worker manager and found it to be extremely quick.
